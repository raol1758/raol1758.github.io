India


I've been to India three times throughout my life. The first time I was just a couple months old, the second time I was ten years old, and the third time I was twelve years old. From cows walking around the streets to the crazy monsoon, the whole experience is very interesting, to say the least.




New York

In the summer of 2016, I went to New York, Long Island with my family. We stayed with some friends and then visited the city. We saw the 9/11 Memorial, the World Trade Center, and the Statue of Liberty.

All About Me!




Heyo, welcome to my first promotional webpage! My name is Anushree Raol, and I am 14 years old.
I like to paint and draw in my free time. Oftentimes, I make acrylic pour paintings.
These are made by mixing water, dish soap, and acrylic paint, and then pouring that onto a blank canvas.
This results in layers of color, which separate to create swirls and cells.
